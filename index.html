I have been calling this project:

Pride Goeth Before the Rebuild


I guess I will start with what I mean by pride.

I built these pages with pride. I built these pages with care. I did it all by myself.

Now I just can't stand them. They've got to go.

I carefully hand-crafted every line of code. Raw HTML, CSS, JavaScript and PHP. No dependencies. Everything from scratch. Often inspired by others, but always worked out and implemented by me, myself and I.

I spent so much time getting everything right, functionally, visually, you name it -- that I didn't ever get to add but a fraction of the actual content I wanted to show here.

So it has to go. I am bringing in Bootstrap, jQuery, Angular, and every other dependency I once eschewed. Well, the ones which will make things go faster and allow me to focus more on the content and style of these pages, instead of just the mechanics.

Right now the pages you are viewing have broken bits. Places where I have stripped away some component in preparation for the new way, the better way. I have opted to provide content, even partially non-functional content, over leaving the old sit operational until I could finish its replacement.

Getting mechanics right is fine, and I love doing so. Unfortunately, unless I point out those bits in code snippets, almost no one is going to be able to appreciate them but the afore-mentioned me, myself and I. While I don't need anyone else's approval, explicit or otherwise, one of the primary reasons I am putting this here is to share.

Since these pages are undergoing a complete rebuild from scratch, so you are likely to encounter the under construction page as you look around. Don't worry, it will be encountered less and less frequently as time goes on. You are also going to encounter the dreaded, "dead link" or two. Not for long, though.

Enjoy looking around while I eat a tremendous slice of humble pie.
