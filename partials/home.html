Pride Goeth Before the Rebuild

I guess I will start with what I mean by pride.

I built these pages with pride. I built these pages with care. I did it all by myself.

Now I just can't stand them. They've got to go.

I carefully hand-crafted every line of code. No dependencies. Everything from scratch. Often inspired by others, but always worked out and implemented by me, myself and I.

I spent so much time getting everything right, functionally, visually, you name it -- that I didn't ever get to add but a fraction of the actual content I wanted to show here.

So it has to go. I am bringing in Bootstrap, jQuery, Angular, and every other dependency I once eschewed. Well, the ones which will make things go faster and allow me to focus more on the content and style of these pages, instead of just the mechanics.

The mechanics are fine, and I love them. Unfortunately, unless I point them out in code snippets, almost no-one is going to be able to appreciate them but the afore-mentioned me, myself and I. While I don't need anyone else's approval, explicit or otherwise, one of the primary reasons I am putting this here is to share.

Since these pages are undergoing a complete rebuild from scratch, so you are likely to encounter the "in progress" page as you look around. Don't worry, it will be encountered less and less frequently as time goes on.
